*How To Use*

  • Check appropriate Radio Button For Typing Scheme.
    • For Chatting Select Chat
    • If Want To Send Email Select Email And Click On Send Email Button After Your Message
  • To Resize Text Area Click And Adjust Bottom Right Symbol
  • Click on Enable Search Button For Starting Speech Recognition
  • To Copy Text Click On Copy Text Button
  • To Send Mail Click onSend Mail Button
  • To Clear Text Box Click On Clear Button
  • To Play Text Audio Click On Play Button
